Cookie Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how rumbla.app uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the site.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies (no consent required)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. The site cannot work without them.

CookiePurposeDuration
rumbla_cookie_consentRemembers whether you have accepted or declined non-essential cookies1 year

Analytics cookies (consent required)

We currently do not use analytics or tracking cookies on rumbla.app. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and request your consent before activating them.

Third-party cookies

rumbla.app does not load third-party advertising or tracking scripts. The interactive map canvas on the landing page may set technical cookies required for map tile rendering — these are considered functional and necessary for the feature to work.

Managing cookies

You can withdraw or change your cookie consent at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the footer of rumbla.app. You can also manage or delete cookies directly through your browser settings — see your browser's help documentation for instructions.

Note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, contact us at info@rumbla.app.
